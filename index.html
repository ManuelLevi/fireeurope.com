FIRE Europe

Europeans can do FIRE too. Here's where to start.

🔥 Start Here

  • 🎥 The Stages of F.I.R.E. (Financial Independence, Retire Early) — This simple video clearly explains different types of FIRE — Regular FIRE, Lean FIRE, Fat FIRE, Barista FIRE, and Coast FIRE.

🧮 Calculators

  • Networthify Calculator — Visualizes your FIRE timeline using simple sliders.
  • The Poor Swiss FIRE Calculator — Swiss-specific, globally relevant withdrawal simulator.
  • Moneyland.ch Calculator 🇨🇭 — Swiss early retirement calculator with local assumptions.
  • cFIREsim — Historical simulation tool for testing retirement plan success. More complex.

🌍 Communities

  • r/EuropeFIRE — The largest FIRE community focused on Europe.
  • Mustachian Post Forum 🇨🇭 — FIRE discussions for Swiss residents and expats.
  • Frugalisten Forum 🇩🇪 — Germany's main FIRE hub focused on frugality and investing.
  • RikaTillsammans Forum 🇸🇪 — Sweden's largest FIRE-oriented finance community.
  • Bogleheads Europe 🇪🇺 — Bogleheads are passive investors who follow Jack Bogle's simple but powerful message to diversify with low-cost index funds and let compounding grow wealth. This section is focused on Non-US investing only.

🎧 Podcasts

  • Financial Independence Europe Podcast — Interviews with FIRE seekers and achievers across Europe.

This site is minimal by design. FIRE isn't about flash — it's about freedom.
